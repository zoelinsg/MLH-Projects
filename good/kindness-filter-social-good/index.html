About This Social Good Filter

This project is a coded filter concept for social media-style content. It is designed to promote kindness, reduce harmful online behavior, and encourage users to share messages that support healthy digital communities.

How It Supports Social Good

  • Encourages respectful and positive online behavior
  • Raises awareness about bullying and harmful comments
  • Promotes empathy, support, and community care
  • Can be adapted for youth education and digital safety campaigns
